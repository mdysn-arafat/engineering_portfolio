Finite Element Method (FEM) Research Works

This repository contains a collection of both published and ongoing Finite Element Method (FEM) research works. The projects include structural analysis studies, verification cases, and exploratory simulations carried out for academic and professional development purposes.

Each project is organized in separate folders. Please refer to the respective directories for detailed descriptions, analysis files, and results.

Note: Some works are completed and documented, while others are still under development.
