Preliminary Structural Analysis of Offshore Floating Platform

This repository contains an ongoing preliminary structural analysis of an offshore floating platform. The study focuses on evaluating the structural response under dominant load cases during the early design stage.

Preliminary results for the governing load conditions are provided in the Analysis folder. In addition, modal analysis results and other progressive simulation files are included to document the development stages of the work.

This study was originally intended for publication in a Q1/Q2 journal. However, further progress was paused due to the supervisor’s engagement in ongoing PhD research, which limited the continuation of the project at this stage.
